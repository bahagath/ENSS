Awards & Achievements

  • A Grade Mridangam artist in All India Radio and Doordarshan.
  • Author of the book ‘RESOUNDING MRIDANGAM: The Majestic South-Indian Drum’, published internationally in 2021. The book is now part of the syllabus for music students at various universities, including the University of Kerala.
  • Recipient of the prestigious “Chembai Trust Award” (2006).
  • Recipient of the Junior Fellowship (2011-12) and Senior Scholarship (1996-97) from the Department of Culture, Government of India.
  • Recipient of the “Pathiramkulangara Devi Chaithanyashree Puraskar” (2019).
  • Received ‘Special Honour’ from Parakkadathu Koyikkal Trust and Sree Poornathrayeesa Sangeetha Sabha (2021) for publishing RESOUNDING MRIDANGAM.
  • First prize and gold medal in Mridangam at the Inter-University National Youth Festival (Gulburga, 1995).
  • Winner in Mridangam at Kerala University Youth Festival for four consecutive years (1993-1997).
  • Served as a member in the panel of judges for Mridangam in Kerala State Schools Youth Festival for several years.
